# Particle Physics · Interactive

**Quarks, leptons, and the Standard Model**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 5 · Research Frontier

## What You'll Learn

- Relativistic kinematics and Feynman diagrams
- Quantum electrodynamics
- Strong interactions and QCD
- Weak interactions and electroweak unification
- The Higgs mechanism
- Neutrino physics and beyond the Standard Model

## References

- David Griffiths, *Introduction to Elementary Particles* (2nd ed., Wiley)
- Mark Thomson, *Modern Particle Physics* (Cambridge)
- Francis Halzen & Alan D. Martin, *Quarks and Leptons* (Wiley)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
